Seven-Star Virtue: Patience — Sterope

Noah's journey embodies the Sixth Star — enduring a hundred years of ridicule to complete a task no one else believed in. He built not from certainty, but from obedience. Patience is the covenant before the rain falls.

  • 🕊️The Dove — the first messenger of peace; a type of the Holy Spirit descending on the faithful.
  • 🌈The Rainbow — God's signature on the covenant; a promise written in light across the sky.
  • ⚓The Ark — engineering as an act of worship; obedience made manifest in wood and pitch.
  • ⏳One Hundred Years — faith endures beyond ridicule; Noah built what others could not imagine.
